Kaidhu Baba

Words and Music (c) Cygnet Repu

A song about diving for trochus mollusks (also known as pearl shell) from the sailing lugger Black Swan
Written by Cygnet Repu from Mabuig Island and recorded on a compendium album of songs from that area.
Trochus is a large mollusk with a conical upper shell, used for the irridescence of its shell and for meat.
It can be very dangerous work.
