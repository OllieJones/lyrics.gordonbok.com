Capt. Dave's Delight

            © Jim Stewart

           

            Jim Stewart, of Saint John, NB made this hornpipe for Capt. Dave Kennedy a few years ago.  Carol Rohl (the harper of Odivere) and I play it on harp and cellamba.  (It was Carol who programmed this album, by the way.)

 

 

Capt. Dave's Delight is recorded on Schooners and is  also in the songbook One to Sing, One to Haul
