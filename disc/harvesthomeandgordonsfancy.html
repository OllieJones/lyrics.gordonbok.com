Harvest Home and Gordon's Fancy

Trad, arranged by Bok

         I learned "Harvest Home" from Truck Croteau ; the second tune is my own. It's easier than it sounds, really; you just pretend you're frailing the banjo. You don't need to know how to play the banjo , either (I don't), nor do you really need a thumb on your right hand, for that matter./i>

Harvest Home and Gordon's Fancy is recorded on the CD Jeremy Brown and Jeannie Teal
